Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes, we offer white-glove delivery services across Nigeria. Delivery is complimentary for orders over ‚Ç¶2,000,000. Our team will handle assembly and placement with care.

What is your warranty policy?

All pieces come with a 5-year structural guarantee. We also offer lifetime replacement of components and complimentary maintenance advice. Custom pieces have extended warranties.

Can you customize furniture?

Absolutely. We offer full customization on colors, materials, dimensions, and finishes. Custom pieces typically take 8-12 weeks. We'll schedule a consultation with our design team.

Do you accept returns?

We have a 30-day return policy for most items. Custom and special orders are non-returnable. Please contact our customer service team for specifics on your purchase.

How do I schedule a design consultation?

Fill out the form above or call +234 (0) 123 456 7890. Our design consultants offer free in-home consultations to assess your space and recommend pieces. VIP services include 3D renderings.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, credit/debit cards, and offer installment plans up to 12 months for qualifying purchases. Corporate and bulk orders may have custom payment terms.
